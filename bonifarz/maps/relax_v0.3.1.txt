"RElax" is a minimalistic time-trial course for "Red Eclipse" (www.redeclipse.net), designed for noobs, by a noob. This is unofficial content.

The intention is to provide a light-colored, recreative and simplistic environment to hone ones basic skills in absence of deadly traps.
